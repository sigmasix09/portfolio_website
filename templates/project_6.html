Multi Disc Clutch Project

"Clutch? oh, in the motorcycles and cars". This was my knowledge when till i designed it by myself. Its not much now also but comparatively better nowadays. Its device used for power transmission and one of the essential components for vehicles that we utilize in our daily lives.


Designing a Multi Disc Clutch was a new learning for me theoretically. As fore-mentioned, I knew very little about clutch so I had to complete my homework before starting the design process. A few videos and literature helped me catch hold of clutch working. While assembling the components, each one of them just fell into the right place. In the end, I learned why a clutch is important and how its designed too.
