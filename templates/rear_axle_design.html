Rear Axle Design

Yet another essential component of automobiles is the Rear Axle. It transmits power to the rear wheels and also allow them to rotate at different speeds by using a differential. The design of the rear axle was quite new to me when I started but I learned more & more along the way.


The rear axle design and assembly would have taught me much with the help of my friends that were in the Automobile Engineering course. I started reading about axle and its working, also asked a few friends to make myself familiar with the assembly that I was dedicated to complete. This project was quite engaging similar to the other projects that I completed and gave me the opportunity to learn something new.
